Lindsay Lohan Almost Bares Breasts, Ditches Engagement Ring

Publish date: 2024-09-24

Lindsay Lohan has a lot on her mind, so maybe that’s why she didn’t notice that she almost bared her breasts in an ill-fitting halter top while out with a friend in Sardinia on Saturday, July 30.

The Mean Girls star came close to having a wardrobe malfunction in the black halter bikini top, but even more revealing was the fact that she has taken off the the emerald engagement ring given to her by estranged fiancé Egor Tarabasov, and moved it to her right hand.

Lohan, 30, fled to the Mediterranean last week after an incident with the Russian businessman that led to police being called to her London apartment as Lohan reportedly stood on her balcony and claimed that the 23-year-old tried to strangle her.

The troubled star, who has been in rehab multiple times, also claimed in a tweet on July 22 that she is pregnant, but has been seen drinking and smoking as she hung out with friends on a yacht in Italy.

Her pal socialite Hofit Golan, who is vacationing with Lohan, told Us on Friday that the former child star is not pregnant.

“We’ve been doing acupuncture, massage, stretching, drinking lots of green juice, exploring caves, fishing, swimming, just doing normal things,” Golan told Us. “Lindsay and I have known each other for years and she’s a very close friend; she’s loyal and an amazing person. It’s unfortunate that other people in her life — I’m not going to name names — are confirming she’s pregnant, which she’s not.”

Lohan’s father, Michael, previously told Us that he believed his daughter was pregnant.

“She’s saying she is, so I believe her,” the dad of seven, 56, told Us on July 26. “She told me that she was.”

While Lohan is relaxing with pals and soaking up the sun, Tarabasov remains in London. He was seen moving his belongings out of the Freaky Friday actress’ apartment earlier this week and was spotted partying at London’s Ciltern Firehouse on Thursday, July 28, according to TMZ.

Golan told Us that things might not be over between Lohan and her younger man. 

“Lindsay is taking a pause in her relationship and joined my friends and I on holiday,” Golan said. “All relationships have ups and downs, and that is what is happening with Lindsay and Egor. They are going through personal matters, but it doesn’t mean they’ve broken up for good. Lindsay just needed a pause.”
