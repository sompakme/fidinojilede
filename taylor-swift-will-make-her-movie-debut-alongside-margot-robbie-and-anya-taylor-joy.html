Taylor Swift Will Make Her Movie Debut Alongside Margot Robbie And Anya Taylor-Joy!

Publish date: 2024-09-14

The award-winning singer-songwriter has been cast in Oscar-nominated filmmaker David O. Russell's new untitled film, alongside a star cast including Margot Robbie, Anya Taylor-Joy, Christian Bale, Rami Malek and many others.

Taylor Swift To Make Her Big Movie Debut

The 11-time Grammy winner will make her big-screen debut in the anticipated period film, but details about her role are still being kept under wraps.

The film boasts of a major star cast with the likes of Oscar-winner Rami Malek, Malcolm and Marie actor John David Washington, The Queen's Gambit star Anya Taylor-Joy and Suicide Squad veteran Margot Robbie.

This is Swift's first big movie role, but she has previously been a part of smaller roles much like her stint in the 2019 musical film Cats. Despite having a single dialogue, Swift made a memorable appearance as the feline femme fatale , Bombalurina. The singer is a self-proclaimed cat lady and was reportedly paid $3 million for her role.

Collider reported that the Instagram celebrity gossip account Deuxmoi was the first platform to share that the Evermore singer would be involved in the upcoming project. The film is backed by 20th Century Studios and New Regency, and is Russell's first film in seven years, since Joy, starring Jennifer Lawrence and Bradley Cooper.

Taylor Swift also starred in two documentaries which were released in 2020. The first is a Netflix featurette focusing on her life as Miss Americana, and the second is titled Folklore: The Long Pond Studio Sessions, which the singer has directed and produced.

Taylor Swift is one of the many singers to have been cast in movie roles this year. Lady Gaga will be seen alongside Adam Driver in House of Gucci, Harry Styles is leading the romantic drama film My Policeman and Louis Tomlinson is being considered for a role in a biopic! Ariana Grande too will be featured in Netflix's Don't Look Up!

Other actors that will join Swift in the American Hustle director's film include Zoe Saldana, Chris Rock, Mike Myers, Robert De Niro, Michael Shannon, Timothy Olyphant, Andrea Riseborough, Matthias Schoenaerts and Alessandro Nivola.
