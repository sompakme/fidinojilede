Taylor Swift is known for her large squad of friends, often showing up to high-profile events with an entire posse of famous faces, just like she did at the Super Bowl to support Travis Kelce. But according to a new report, not all of Swift’s friends get along very well.

Speaking to Life and Style, a source claims that Blake Lively, 36, and Selena Gomez, 31, refuse to be in the same room together, as they get on so poorly, creating an awkward situation for Taylor, as she can’t invite them to the same get-togethers.

Taylor Is Apparently Tired Of Selena And Blake’s Drama

“Taylor’s tried many times to broker peace between these two divas, but they won’t play nice,” they explained. “She thinks it’s silly the way they carry on, especially since the two don’t even know where all this hating began!”

While it appears Taylor has been doing her best to accommodate her friends, the source cautions that she’s getting fed up of the situation, hinting the feud between Selena and Blake may interfere with their friendships with Swift.

“Taylor feels like she’s doing a juggling act in a circus to keep them both happy, but her patience is wearing thin,” the source continued.

Selena Is Allegedly Jealous Blake Is Taylor’s Party Friend

Taylor has been friend with Selena for a longer time, both meeting in 2008 and hanging out regularly after that, as they were both making a name for themselves in the entertainment industry. Blake and Taylor first connected in 2015 while Lively was at the height of her Gossip Girl success. Now, Taylor has gone on to write songs referencing the daughters Blake shares with her husband Ryan Reynolds, showing just how close the two women are.

The same report from Life and Style says that Taylor and Blake enjoy each other’s company when they’re both down to party, whereas Taylor relies on Selena for more serious support and conversations. But apparently, Selena wishes she could have the same type of relationship with Taylor as Blake does, often appearing left out when partying is involved.

“Truth is, Selena’s insanely jealous of Taylor’s friendship with Blake,” the insider further explained. “Blake was on hand to help Taylor celebrate her birthday in December, and Selena was seething about it for weeks.”

To conclude, the source again iterated that Taylor may be forced to pick between Selena and Blake in the near future, as she’s struggling to manage both friendships. “Selena and Blake are extremely petty sometimes and it stresses out Taylor,” they explained. “Either they start getting along or one of them will have to go!”
