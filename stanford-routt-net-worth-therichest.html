Stanford Bermond Routt has an estimated net worth of $18 million. Currently inactive in the NFL, this athlete spent seven seasons with the Oakland Raiders, from 2005 through 2011. He has also played for the Houston Texans and the Kansas City Chiefs.

While studying at the University of Houston, Routt has excelled both in football as well as indoor and outdoor track. He finished a degree in Kinesiology with concentration in sports administration.

In 2005, he was selected by the Oakland Raiders as the second round 38th overall draft pick. He then spent the next few years with the Raiders. In 2011, he was signed by the team to a three-year $31.5 million contract extension. However, before the 2012 season commenced, the team released him.

He managed to ink a deal with the Kansas City Chiefs in February 2012. He was signed to a three-year $19.6 million contract but was released by the Chiefs in November of the same year. He also had a short stint with the Houston Texans.
