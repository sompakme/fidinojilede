Kim Kardashian Meets With Hillary Clinton And Jeff Bezos In The Same Week

Publish date: 2024-09-11

Kim Kardashian and Pete Davidson seem to be loving the company of some high profile personalities as they were seen heading out for a dinner party at the Los Angeles residence of Amazon founder, Jeff Bezos. It was reported by TMZ that Kim and Pete rode together in Kim’s car to head over to Bezos mansion where they spent hours and were later seen arriving at Davidson's Beverly Hills hotel. It was still unclear whether they were the only guests at Bezos home or they were with other famous personalities.

The details of the dinner party held at Bezos’ mansion were not known as of this writing but it has been one of the most recent hangout session of the couple following their previous sighting where the couple was seen holding hands while grabbing some pizza from the Jon & Vinny's in Los Angeles. Their romance seems to be getting much more serious and their meeting with Bezos seems to indicate something is brewing.

The recent sighting of the couple broke just days after Kim was seen having a coffee meet with Hillary Clinton and her daughter Chelsea Clinton. It was reported by People that the meet up between them was in regards to the upcoming Apple TV+ series of the Clintons which will feature some diverse cast of empowered women. The three women were seen meeting at the Canoga Park neighborhood of Los Angeles.

The coffee shop that the trio went to is Hot & Cool Café which was Black-owned and vegan café which reportedly hires some of the formerly incarcerated people as well as kids from the community. This is definitely a go to place for Kardashian who worked hard to free some selected incarcerated people whose stories had inspire her.

Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum meeting the Clintons can be seen as bit more casual unlike her previous visit to the White House. The SKIMS founder just wore shiny black trench coat with a lime green thigh-high boots which she paired with a black sunglasses. Kim paired her outfit with a black leather gloves and just carrying a tiny black bag. Hillary on the other hand was seen wearing her signature pantsuits which she paired with gray number with a black blouse and Chelsea was wearing a coordinated outfit with her mom which was a black sweater and charcoal skirt.
