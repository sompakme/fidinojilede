10 Cutest Sibling Relationships In Hollywood

Publish date: 2024-08-23

There are plenty of cute celebrity couples, but nothing is more sweeter than the strong relationships between celebrity siblings. Stars love sharing quality time with their siblings, and we've seen them taking their brothers and sisters to their red carpet events, hitting the gym, and taking endless amounts of selfies on Instagram.

RELATED: 10 Photos Of Celebs With Their Lookalike Sisters

We love to see stars like Gigi, Bella, and Anwar Hadid walk red carpets together or supporting each other's careers on social media, and sisters Elle and Dakota Fanning, who are both actresses, praising one another on their movie roles. These celeb siblings have strong bonds that no one can ever break.

This list shares the 10 cutest sibling relationships in Hollywood. Some siblings have been fortunate enough to both find success in movies and television and fans love seeing them come together.

10 Vanessa And Stella Hudgens

Vanessa Hudgens has been in countless movies, including High School Musical, Beastly, Spring Breakers, and The Knight Before Christmas. She's dated Hollywood stars like Zac Efron and recently broke up with long time boyfriend Austin Butler, but what most people tend to forget is that she also has a younger sister, Stella, who looks identical to the actress, is just as worthy of the spotlight.

RELATED: Vanessa Hudgens Teases New Character In 'The Princess Switch: Switched Again'

Hudgens' younger sister of six years, Stella Hudgens, is a model, actress, and social media influencer. While Vanessa may have amplified her fame, the 25-year-old brunette beauty has over one million Instagram followers herself. The sisters bond is very strong with Stella even traveling across the country to surprise her older sister by hiding in a large box and wishing her a happy birthday. It was a touching moment caught on Stella's Instagram.

9 Gigi, Bella, And Anwar Hadid

Gigi and Bella Hadid are known as two of the most successful models in the world, and their sisterly bond is so sweet, they've walked runways together and have starred in the same campaigns together for top designers like Balmain and Moschino.

Everyone knows how close these two sisters are, but they are also extremely close with their younger brother Anwar Hadid. While he isn't in the spotlight like his two extremely successful sisters, he supports his siblings on red carpet events and loves sharing throwback photos of them.

8 Nicole and Sofia Richie

Nicole and Sofia Richie have an 18 year age difference, but that hasn't hindered their sisterly bond one bit.  In fact, the two Hollywood stars are very close and have been a lot closer after Sofia and Scott Disick decided to end their relationship for good.

RELATED: Everything Scott Disick Has Done Since His Split From Kourtney Kardashian

According to ScreenRant, while Nicole and their father, Lionel Richie were hesitant about Sofia's relationship with Kourtney Kardashian's ex, they came to terms with it. But, once the relationship fizzled, Nicole, like the big supportive sister she is, came to Sofia's side. "Nicole and Sofia have been spending more time together since Sofia started spending less time with Scott," a source told Cosmopolitan. 

7 Chris, Liam, And Luke Hemsworth

These three handsome Australian men have a super-tight brotherly bond and are all talented in their own right. Chris, Liam, and Luke Hemsworth are all actors, and before they all really made it big, they actually all starred on the same Australian soap opera together called Neighbours. 

The brothers all look up to one another, with Luke posting a touching Instagram photo of younger brother Chris with the caption, "You're a constant inspiration and beacon of joy." Liam, the youngest of the three brothers, also looks up to his brother Chris, sharing, "I'm thankful that I have him and can use his resources and his knowledge. I call him all the time about scripts and get his opinion." The bond these three men share is unmatched in Hollywood.

6 Jake And Maggie Gyllenhaal

One of the most well-known Hollywood sibling relationships is between actors Jake and Maggie Gyllenhaal. The pair are known for their incredible work in movies and both stars have actually found their breakout roles in dark films.

RELATED: The Truth About Joey King's Relationship With Her Sisters

Older sister Maggie was discovered after her role in the dark comedy Secretary, while younger brother Jake is known for his role in the cult classic Donnie Darko. The two stars are one of the most supportive siblings in Hollywood, too. Most famously, they were both nominees at the 2015 Golden Globes, going as each other's date, and Maggie even won best actress in a mini-series for The Honorable Woman. 

5 Derek And Julianne Hough

Another Hollywood pair of very supportive siblings is Derek and Julianne Hough, regarded as one of the most iconic sibling pairs on television. Both siblings have very similar career paths as amazing dancers and performers, with both Derek and Julianne working on Dancing With the Stars, and working on musicals, with Derek involved in Hairspray Live! and Julianne playing Sandy on Grease Live. 

The Hough are majorly talented people with Julianne also an Emmy Award winner, singer, and actor, while brother Derek has been a judge on World of Dance, a two-time Emmy Award winner, and an Olympic dance choreographer.

4 Dakota And Elle Fanning

Dakota and Elle Fanning could almost be twins, with both women finding success in Hollywood as top actresses. Both Dakota and Elle began their careers at a very young age, with Dakota starring in I Am Sam at the age of eight and Elle starring as a younger version of her sister's character in the same film.

Both sisters have been greatly supportive of one another and Dakota even gushed about their special bond with Elle in an issue of Shape. "My sister, Elle, and I are really close. We always have been. We're very different, but we're very connected. We don't talk every day, but we don't have to. We just have that special bond," she touchingly shared, adding, "There's no one I want to succeed more in this business than my sister, and I know she wants the same for me."

3 Nikki And Nathan Reed

Nikki Reed may be the famous sibling in the Reed family, but she shares a very close relationship with her brother Nathan. The two are so close, they are known for showing off their acrobatic yoga skills on Instagram.

Back in 2017, Nikki Reed shared just how much she fell in love with acro yoga, with her partner being none other than her brother, Nathan. "I'm going to say this now: I'm determined to become the most badass brother-sister acro yoga duo on the planet." The pair have shared multiple Instagram videos of their yoga skills and they are pretty impressive. And, not only has Nikki and Nathan bonded over yoga, but the two have also been each other's dates to Nikki's movie events and red carpets.

2 Lupita And Peter Nyong'o

Actress Lupita Nyong'o and her brother Peter "Junio" Nyong'o are extremely close. We first met her younger brother at the 2014 Oscars when he got in on that famous selfie taken by Bradley Cooper which also had stars like Jennifer Lawrence, Angelina Jolie, and Brad Pitt all together for the perfect shot.

While we haven't seen her brother in any major movies, we're sure he'll be following in his sister's footsteps. Lupita even said so herself while talking to Ellen DeGeneres on her show. "He's quite the performer!" The actress shared. This summer, Lupita even shared a touching Instagram post of her brother getting married even though she wasn't able to attend amid the coronavirus. The two stars are very supportive and we love to see it!

1 Dylan And Cole Sprouse

One of the most famous siblings in Hollywood is twin brothers Dylan and Cole Sprouse. The two got their start working together in Adam Sandler's movie Big Daddy, and from 2005-2008 starred on the Disney Channel sitcom The Suite Life of Zack & Cody, which made these two young stars into heartthrobs.

According to MSN, at the end of 2000, the twin brothers became the richest teenage twins in the world. In 2018, Cole went on to star in Riverdale as Jughead Jones, while brother Dylan is a co-owner of All-Wise Meadery in Brooklyn. However, we hope to see them working in Hollywood together again.
