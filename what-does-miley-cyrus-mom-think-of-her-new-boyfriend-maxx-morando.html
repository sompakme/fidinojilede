Miley Cyrus' Relationship Baggage Prior To Meeting Maxx Morando

Though she constantly basks in the success of her musical endeavors, Miley, who recently quit being vegan, also tried her hand at acting. In 2010, Miley was cast in the film The Last Song, a movie based on the Nicholas Sparks book of the same name. During this time, she met fellow actor Liam Hemsworth, who played her love interest in the movie. The pair hit it off quite well, both on-screen and off-screen.

Soon, rumors about Miley and Liam in a relationship started to take over the internet. The couple finally confirmed that they got into a relationship in 2010. Since then, the pair had been in an on-and-off relationship. They finally announced their engagement in June 2012. However, the following year, Miley and Liam called it quits without citing a specific reason.
