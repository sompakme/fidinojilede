Cameron Diaz Gives Rare Update On Daughters Relationship With Benji Madden

Publish date: 2024-07-22

Highlights

  • Cameron Diaz opens up about her daughter's relationship with Benji Madden, revealing that he sings to her and she knows the words to his songs.
  • Despite their privacy, Cameron and Benji announced the birth of their daughter, Raddix, in January 2020 and expressed their joy in becoming parents.
  • Cameron Diaz returned to acting after a hiatus and starred in the Netflix film Back in Action, though it's unclear if her comeback will be temporary or if she has more projects planned.

Cameron Diaz is notoriously private when it comes to her family, especially her daughter, Raddix, as no photos have ever been publicly released featuring the toddler. But Cameron gave fans a rare update about her daughter’s relationship with her dad, rocker Benji Madden.

During an appearance on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon, which aired on October 25, Cameron shared how her husband bonds with their 3-year old.

Cameron Says Her Daughter Loves Hearing Benji Madden Sing

“He has, like, the best songs,” the actress explained. “They play in my head all the time because he sings them to her and she knows the words, and it’s the sweetest thing ever. It really is. It kills me.”

“He has some bangers,” she went on. “I’m telling you. I’m like, ‘Whoa, that is like, that’s catchy.’”

Benji and Cameron have been married since 2015. They announced their daughter’s arrival in January 2020 via social media. The public was unaware the couple had been expecting up until then.

“Happy New Year from the Maddens! We are so happy, blessed and grateful to begin this new decade by announcing the birth of our daughter, Raddix Madden,” the couple wrote in the post. “She has instantly captured our hearts and completed our family.”

There’s been speculation that the couple used a surrogate to welcome their baby, given that images of Cameron pregnant have never been revealed as well as the fact she was in her mid-40s when Raddix arrived.

Cameron Un-Retired From Acting, But Still Keeps Her Personal Life Private

Though it’s been three years since Raddix’s birth, Cameron and Benji haven’t shared any images of their daughter online nor have the paparazzi captured shots of the child.

On August 30, Benji posted a sweet tribute to his wife on her 51st birthday, giving a rare insight into their nearly decade-long relationship. “So today, it’s a very Happy Birthday to my beautiful wife, best friend, best partner in everything, ride or die, greatest Mom to our little girl, @Avaline wine boss, family chef, advisor, collaborator and everything else," he wrote in the touching post.

In response, the Charlie’s Angel actress shared a sweet message in the comments. “There’s no one else I would rather do this life with. We are so blessed... I you with all my,” she wrote.
