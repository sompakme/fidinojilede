Like Britney Spears and Justin Timberlake or Nick Lachey and Jessica Simpson, Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck were one of those 2000s celebrity pairs who seemed like a perfect match. It was terrible to learn about J. Lo and Affleck's 2004 split and thrilling to watch them fall back in love many decades later. Since Affleck and Lopez remained friendly, it's no wonder that they not only reunited but got married.

Music superstar Lopez and Affleck starred in the 2003 movie Gigli together, and they started their relationship after realizing how well they got along. While fans know a lot about Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck's romance since they have been such a beloved couple for so long, they might not have heard the story of their very first kiss.

What Did Jennifer Lopez And Ben Affleck Say About Their First Kiss?

In 2003, when the couple was engaged, Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck talked about Gigli in an interview with Access Hollywood. The pair answered a question about the first time that they kissed.

The couple shared that their first kiss happened on camera since their Gigli characters kissed. When asked to describe that moment, Affleck got a little flustered and didn't seem to know what to say at first. The reporter said:

"People are going to watch this movie and try to figure out if you're in love."

Affleck agreed that fans would be interested in watching Gigli "for that reason" and understood it "was part of the attraction." He said he was mostly thinking about "trying to make the scene work, trying to make it good." Although the actor has since made some comments about the movie that suggest that he didn't end up loving it, he sounded proud of it at the time.

While Lopez and Affleck looked content in their Access Hollywood interview, they have both shared that as their relationship got more serious, they found all the attention from the public difficult.

In 2023, Lopez told Variety they felt "PTSD" and added, "we’re older now. We’re wiser. We also know what’s important, what’s really important in life, and it’s not so much what other people think. It’s about being true to who you are.”

Jennifer Lopez And Ben Affleck Revealed More About Their Early Relationship In Their Access Hollywood Interview

Since Jennifer Lopez was married when she and Ben Affleck starred in Gigli, the beginning of their romance sounds like it was a bit complicated. According to Insider.com, the couple got engaged in 2002 but broke up in 2004.

Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck talked a lot about the early days of their relationship in their interview with Access Hollywood. They answered a question about their first meeting, which took place at the inaugural read-through of Gigli. When asked about when they developed feelings for each other, Affleck said:

"You can't pinpoint that. We became friends first."

Affleck added that they developed a friendship in the beginning and said, "Because the whole thing was totally off limits, you know, we did end up becoming friends... When you're not trying to impress somebody because you don't think that you're ever going to be dating them..."
