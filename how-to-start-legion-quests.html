How to start legion quests

Publish date: 2024-08-21

How do you start the Legion quest in Shadowlands?

Use the Adventure’s Board and click Fight the legion. That was it, tyvm. If you teleport to Dalaran there will be a dude that spawns in and starts a quest line for you.

How do I start the quest Legion in Dalaran?

Starting Legion Questing: Broken Shore, Dalaran, Artifacts, Professions. Before you can quest through the Broken Isles, you first need to run through the Broken Shore (or Mardum if a Demon Hunter), witness Dalaran moving to the Broken Isles, acquire your Artifact Weapon, and unlock your Class Hall.

How do I unlock legion quests?

In order to unlock Broken Isles World Quests, you must complete the Uniting the Isles quest, which should automatically pop up for your character at level 45. If it does not for some reason, or you are level 50 or higher, visit Khadgar in the Violet Citadel in Legion Dalaran to pick up the quest manually.

How do I start the quest Broken Shore?

First you have to go to Dalaran (Broken Isles), go to the Violet Citadel and speak to Khadgar there. Take the quest Uniting the Isles. Turn in to Khadgar in front of you, then make your way to Krasus’ Landing. Khadgar is now here, and will give you Armies of Legionfall.

What level can you start legion?

If your character is level 50 or above, visit the Hero’s Call Board in Stormwind or the Warchief’s Command Board in Orgrimmar and pick up the Broken Shore option “Fight The Legion“. This will grant you the quest “The Legion Returns” (Alliance/Horde).

Can you skip Legion intro quests?

Talk to the NPC in front of you to skip the Legion introductory quests and head straight to Dalaran.
