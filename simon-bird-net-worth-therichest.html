Simon Bird is an English comedian and actor best recognized for playing the role of Will McKenzie on the sitcom The Inbetweeners, as well as in Friday Night Dinner, as Adam Goodman.

Simon Antony Bird was born on August 19, 1984 in Guildford, Surrey, England. After studying at the Royal Grammar School, Guildford, Bird attended Queens' College, Cambridge.

Bird's appearance on The Inbetweeners in 2008 won him the Best Male Newcomer award at the 2008 British Comedy Awards. He was also named Best Actor at the 2009 British Comedy Awards. His impressive portrayal of the role in the sitcom earned him a Best Comedy Performance nomination at the Royal Television Society Awards in 2008. He was also nominated as the Best Male Performance in a Comedy Programme at the BAFTA Awards in 2009.

After The Inbetweeners, Simon Bird has also appeared in other TV shows like The King is Dead, Comedy Showcase, Friday Night Dinner and Chickens. In 2013, he appeared in films like The Look of Love and The Harry Hill Movie.  In 2014, he is expected to appear in a second The Inbetweeners movie.
