Liv Teverino, a High School Student of Georgia; Died In a Car Crash

Publish date: 2024-10-01

A 16-year-old high school student, Liv Teverino, passed away in an automobile accident In Georgia on Monday evening, September 18, 2023. According to WANF, Liv Teverino was a passenger in a car that slammed into a tree in Marietta County and caught fire.

The firefighters immediately got a call that Liv’s car was engulfed in flames, and she was declared deceased due to this tragic accident; her death news was released on social media on September 19.

Grant Rivera, the superintendent of Marietta City Schools, stated that the 16-year-old was a junior at Marietta High School. Liv was a leader in her community, an athlete, and a scholar.

Dawn White confirmed her death news through a Facebook post and wrote;

“16-year-old RAYERS At Marietta High School, Liv Teverino was a standout athlete who graduated at the top of her class. She was also a devoted friend to everyone she encountered.

The teen’s daily parking spot outside the high school is now covered in a sea of flowers and notes scrawled in chalk by her classmates. Oliver Crowson, a junior, claimed that the location, which is often joyful, has turned into a gathering of grief.”

Thoughts and Condolences to her family, friends and school community in this trying time; Liv’s sudden demise left the community in mourning and loss.

Dawn also wrote in her Facebook statement that she moved everyone she spoke to or passed by. Liv was a model Christian; she was knowledgeable. She was likely aiming for valedictorian.

Now, students said that Teverino’s character should serve as a model for positively impacting people’s lives. We should all strive to live like her. Live kindly and sweetly,” Crowson advised.

Liv personified determination and passion. Liv thrived, leaving an imprint of excellence, according to Rivera. “Liv was selected to be a member of this year’s leadership cohort because she is recognized as a community leader outside of school.

Prayer Vigil

Marietta FUMC shared a candlelight prayer vigil ceremony in remembrance of Liv Teverino. The Vigil Cemenoy will be held on Wednesday, September 20, 2023, at 7:00 p.m. and last at 8:00 p.m.

Everyone is invited to attend the Vigil Prayer in memory of Liv at Park on Polk Pavillion at Marietta First UMC; the post shared on Facebook stated:

“You are cordially invited to join us for a Candlelight Prayer Vigil tomorrow, Wednesday, September 20, at 7:00 p.m. at Marietta First UMC. As a community, we will gather to pray and remember Liv Teverino, a dear friend and student.”

Prayers for the young girl. May her soul get peace afterlife. Rest in Peace, LIV TEVERINO.
