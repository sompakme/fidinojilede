2021 RAM 1500 TRX - $72,120

Richard Rawlings has recently added a 2021 RAM 1500 TRX to his impressive collection. 1970 Dodge Challenger R/T - $86,805

The 1970 Dodge Challenger R/T is considered by many to be one of the most iconic muscle cars of all time. The Challenger R/T boasts a powerful 7.2-liter V8 engine that produces 390 horsepower as per auto evolution. Its timeless design, iconic shaker hood scoop, and bold front grille make it a classic that still turns heads today. Richard's Challenger R/T truly represents his love for classic American muscle cars, and it's no wonder that it's one of his favorite cars in his collection.

1986 Rolls-Royce Corniche Convertible - $163,800

One of Richard Rawlings's most prized possessions, the 1986 Rolls-Royce Corniche convertible embodies luxury. 1968 Ford Shelby Mustang - $396,000
