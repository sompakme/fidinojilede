Review: Admire My Skin - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-09-02

What is Admire My Skin?

Admire My Skin is a skincare brand dedicated to providing affordable and effective solutions for various skin issues. It all started when Amy Romero, the co-founder and president, struggled with cystic acne and melasma for years. After spending a fortune on prescription creams, washes, and antibiotics, she decided to help develop more affordable options for people facing similar challenges. This journey led her to create Admire My Skin, a brand that redefines skincare and empowers individuals to confidently embrace their skin.

Today, the company offers a range of products for blemishes, pigmentation, and aging concerns. They also offer a variety of skin-brightening solutions, including serums and creams that target dark spots and sun damage. Quality and innovation lie at the heart of Admire My Skin. Each product is carefully crafted in an FDA-certified lab in the United States, ensuring the highest quality and effectiveness.

But it doesn't stop there — Admire My Skin is also committed to cruelty-free practices, harming no animals in creating their products. This dedication to ethical and compassionate practices is a testament to the brand's values and vision.

Admire My Skin keeps up to date on various social media platforms, including Facebook, Instagram, and Pinterest. This online presence allows Admire My Skin to connect with its customers, showcase its products, and shares skincare tips and inspiring stories of satisfied customers who have regained their confidence through Admire My Skin's transformative products.

Admire My Skin Reviews from Customers

After checking out Admire My Skin's official store and its Amazon storefront, we were instantly impressed by the range of products and the positive customer reviews. Many customers reported positive results after using Admire My Skin products, such as improved skin texture, reduced dark spots, and diminished fine lines and wrinkles.

One customer, who has been regularly using its Potent Retinoid Cream, mentioned that the product works very well for them, as their facial spots are slowly but noticeably fading. They also appreciated that it was not harsh on their skin. Another customer shared that they apply the product in the evening, and their skin still feels moisturized in the morning. They also noticed the added benefit of smoothing small wrinkles and found it easy to apply.

A customer who has long battled severely dry and eczema-prone skin their entire life shared that this oil has made a significant difference. After trying numerous creams and remedies, they found that the Vitamin C Oil cleared and smoothed their skin, eliminating itchy red patches. Another customer stated that it was their first time trying the product, and they noticed it moisturizes their skin, gives a natural glow, and helps clear up black patches and hyperpigmentation. They highly recommend it and plan to purchase more.

Their Intense Hydration Cream also garnered positive reviews, with users praising its ease of use and lightweight formula. One customer on their second bottle claims they're a "lifer" for this product. They have sensitive skin and have experienced reactions from other skincare products, but this cream works wonders for them. It's lightweight and usable as a primer before applying makeup. They also mentioned that a little goes a long way, and one bottle lasts them about three months. Another customer, who owns almost all Admire My Skin products, said their skin looks and feels 10 years younger.

Similarly, the brand's Face Mask is a major hit. They said the face mask helped them clear up their acne, and they like it enough for daily use. One woman claimed that she couldn't stop touching her skin after using this product because it left it feeling so soft and smooth. She also mentioned that she was quite impressed with the fact that it didn't make her face feel oily or sticky.

If you still can’t figure out whether this skincare brand is worth it or not, here are some compelling reasons to give it a try:

  • Admire My Skin offers a variety of skincare products, including moisturizers, toners, serums, and more. They all contain natural ingredients that are safe for sensitive skin types but still effective enough to improve your skin’s health.
  • The products feature potent ingredients that deliver visible results within weeks.
  • The brand remains committed to producing cruelty-free products and creating products without harming animals.
  • All the products are easy to apply and feel light on your skin, so you don’t have to worry about feeling uncomfortable while wearing makeup.
  • Admire My Skin offers competitive prices, making its products accessible to a wider range of customers.
  • They also have an intuitive website, making it simple to shop for your favorite products.

Admire My Skin Shipping and Return Policy

Admire My Skin dedicates itself to providing a seamless shopping experience, which includes a hassle-free shipping and return policy.

Shipping Policy

The brand offers free standard shipping on orders over £21.00 within the United States. Orders ship from the company's Atlanta warehouse within 1 to 2 business days and typically arrive within 3-5 business days using USPS or UPS. For those needing faster delivery, expedited shipping is also available and will typically arrive within 1 to 3 business days, depending on your location. Once your order ships, you will receive a UPS tracking number to monitor its progress and estimated delivery date.

Return Policy

Admire My Skin highly values customer satisfaction. To that end, customers can return products within 60 days of purchase. Your item must be in unused, like new condition and in its original packaging to be eligible for a return. Additionally, the company requires a receipt or proof of purchase to complete your return. To initiate a return, mail your product to 340 Royal Poinciana Way, Palm Beach, Florida, USA 33480. Upon receiving and inspecting your returned item, Admire My Skin will send you an email notification to inform you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, you’ll receive a credit typically within 2-3 business days.
