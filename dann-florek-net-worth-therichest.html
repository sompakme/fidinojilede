American director and actor Dann Florek has an estimated net worth of $10 million in 2013. Ezekial Dann Florek was born on May 1, 1950 Flat Rock, Michigan, USA. He is an alumnus of Eastern Michigan University, though he has ever competed a degree. Eventually, he went to New York to pursue an acting career.

Debuting in 1982, Florek is noted for his recurring appearances in TV shows like L.A. Law (22 episodes), Roseanne, and Law & Order (66 episodes as a main cast member and three episodes as a guest star).

As a producer, he is known for his work on Santorini Blue. From 1993 through 1995, he served as director of three episodes of Law & Order which include "The Pursuit of Happiness", "Big Bang" and "Bad Faith".
