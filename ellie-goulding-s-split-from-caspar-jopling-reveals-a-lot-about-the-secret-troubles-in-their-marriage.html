Ellie Goulding's Split From Caspar Jopling Reveals A Lot About The Secret Troubles In Their Marriage

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Highlights

  • Ellie Goulding and Caspar Jopling's marriage has had its share of difficulties, with reports of a split emerging in June 2023.
  • The couple tried to hide their marital troubles in order to protect their young son.
  • Despite considering divorce, Goulding and Jopling have done everything to make their relationship work.

With a music career spanning nearly two decades and over 30 accolades to her name, English pop sensation Ellie Goulding is one of the most accomplished singers of her generation. But when she's not dominating UK and global music charts, Goulding is a devoted mother to 2-year-old Arthur and a loving wife to art dealer Caspar Jopling.

The two tied the knot back in 2019 in a lavish star-studded ceremony held at the prestigious York Minster Cathedral in England, and welcomed their son shortly thereafter. Sadly, despite their spectacular beginnings, Jopling and Goulding’s marriage has not been devoid of turbulent times. Back in June, the duo made headlines when reports emerged that they’d decided to go their separate ways. Here’s what they’ve let slip about the secret troubles that led to their split.

Inside Ellie Goulding And Caspar Jopling's Marital Issues

Held at England's prestigious York Minster Cathedral in 2019, Ellie Goulding and Caspar Jopling's nuptials were the stuff of dreams. Attended by both celebrity and actual royalty, the wedding seemed like the perfect beginning to a fairytale marriage.

Unfortunately, Jopling and Goulding were married for only four years before reports emerged that their marriage had hit a rough patch. According to The Sun, the marriage had been severely impacted by the couple’s busy schedules, leading to a split in June 2023.

Per an inside source, the twosome's marriage had been on the rocks for a solid six months before their June 2023 split. “Things have been bad for at least six months now,” the source revealed to The Sun, “both parties have been really trying to be happy together for the sake of their son, but these things can only take so long before it is untenable.”

The duo had reportedly gone to great lengths to conceal their marital troubles in a bid to protect their young son. “Ellie's team were insisting that she and Caspar were happy, that any suggestions of them being apart were totally untrue and now this. It has all been very mysterious indeed,” the source added. “There had been suggestions of troubles and even a full on split for quite some time, but they kept insisting everything was totally fine.”

However, as the cracks in the marriage deepened, the two eventually decided to call it quits. “They’ve tried to find a compromise where they continue to share lots of time together for their son Arthur, but outside of that sometimes live separate lives for now.”

Ellie Goulding Grew Close To Politician Zac Goldsmith After Her Split From Caspar Jopling

Following her split from Jopling, Ellie Goulding was spotted in the company of conservative politician Lord Zac Goldsmith. The two were first pictured together in June 2022, but reportedly grew closer in the wake of Goulding’s split from Jopling.

“Ellie and Zac met some time ago and became pals,” an inside source revealed to Mirror UK. “They have been in touch since Ellie and Caspar decided to separate and she has shared what she is going through. Having experienced the breakdown of his own marriage, Zac has been very sympathetic to her experience.”

However, according to Mirror UK, the relationship had nothing to do with the breakdown of Goulding’s marriage to Jopling. “Ellie and Zac’s friendship might seem like an unlikely one, but they have shared interests,” an insider revealed to the outlet. “Zac was sorry to hear that Ellie and Caspar had decided to spend time apart and so, like many of her other friends, apparently offered her help if she needed it.”

Have Ellie Goulding And Caspar Jopling Filed For Divorce?

In the wake of their split, Caspar Jopling and Ellie Goulding continued to share their marital home and co-parent their 2-year-old son, Arthur. Though estranged, they hesitated to file for divorce.

"There is no discussion of a divorce and neither of them have been in a new relationship," a source close to the couple revealed to The Sun back in June.

"The priority is to keep good friends and remain amicable whilst they work out their next steps, so they can stay close and enjoy being mum and dad to Arthur. There will always be a lot of love between them despite some difficult times, and they want the best for each other."

Fortunately, despite being on the precipice of divorce, Jopling and Goulding eventually decided to reconcile, primarily for the sake of their 2-year-old son.

“[Ellie] is determined to make things work with Caspar and is committed to giving things another go,” a source recently revealed to The Sun. “She knows it is the right thing to do for the sake of their young son because she wants them to be a proper family unit,” the source continued. “It has been an incredibly tough year for both Ellie and Caspar, but they have had some very frank conversations and are both on the same page. There is a lot of love between them.”

However, as it turns out, Goulding had to make one very specific compromise to get Jopling on board with the reconciliation; ending her burgeoning relationship with Tory politician Zac Goldsmith. According to The Sun, the ‘Love Me Like You Do’ singer cut off all contact with Goldsmith, going as far as unfollowing him on social media.
