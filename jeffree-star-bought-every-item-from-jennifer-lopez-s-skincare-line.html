Jeffree Star Bought Every Item From Jennifer Lopezs Skincare Line

Publish date: 2024-07-24

YouTuber and professional makeup artist Jeffree Star showed off his shopping cart to fans, full of Jennifer Lopez's new skincare line. How much are the products, and can we expect a lengthy review video from the beauty mogul?

First thing's first, the line is pricey and not made for Lopez's fans on a budget. Even Star addresses that, who lives a lavish life 24/7.

"Let's Get Into It"

"Miss Jennifer Lopez, she's dropped her full motherf*cking skincare line," Star announced on his Instagram stories, "Now the price points, are a little cuckoo, but it's f*cking JLo b*tch. She's an icon!"

Cuckoo is true, as her "That JLo Glow" multitasking serum is $119. Its ingredients include a trademarked JLo Beauty™ Olive Complex, Japanese rice sake ferment, yeast-derived ferment, and niacinamide.

Star continued, "I'm going to spare y'all the waste of time and I'm going to buy them all. I'm going to buy every single product here b*tch. We're going to try them next week or whenever they get here."

He's willing to drop a pretty penny on products he may or may not love because well, he can, but also for the sake of his fans. The honest reviewer of products won't hype up a cleanser simply because a musical sensation's name is attached to it.

Analyzing The Line

Although JLo Beauty appeals to a high-end market, the collection is also on the smaller side. That may hint at more time and care dedicated to each item, ensuring their quality. There are seven products altogether.

Star recorded his online shopping list for Instagram viewers to prove that he indeed bought everything, and is not sponsored by Lopez's team.

Purchasing the entire JLo Beauty Line would total up to a little over $400, not including tax and shipping rates. Star is enticing his fans with the lingering questions of whether the awaited skincare will be a hit or a flop.

JLo followers are delivering mixed reviews about her business venture. Some are bragging about going bankrupt after purchasing her products while others are telling fellow fans to not waste their money.

Will it be the best celebrity moment to arise in the beauty community since Rihanna's FENTY launch? We'll have to wait and see if that true JLo glow shines on in those bronzed bottles.
