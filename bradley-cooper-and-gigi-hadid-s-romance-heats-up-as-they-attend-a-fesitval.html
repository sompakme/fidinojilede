Bradley Cooper And Gigi Hadids Romance Heats Up As They Attend A Fesitval

Publish date: 2024-09-05

Highlights

  • Bradley Cooper and Gigi Hadid were seen at a festival dancing and holding hands, fueling rumors of a serious relationship.
  • Cooper joined Eddie Vedder onstage to sing, while Hadid showed support. Videos of them dancing together have gone viral.
  • Sources suggest that Cooper and Hadid have bonded over similarities, like being single parents, and enjoy their time together.

Bradley Cooper and Gigi Hadid have been spending a lot of time together recently. The couple were spotted at the BottleRock Napa Valley festival earlier this week. Onlookers saw them dancing to Stevie Nicks' set, Us Weekly reports. The publication also notes how they were holding hands while dancing. This recent outing adds to the claims that the pair are serious.

The festival was also an important moment for the actor, who got to sing onstage alongside Pearl Jam's Eddie Vedder. They performed the song, Maybe It's Time, and Hadid was there to support him.

Gigi Hadid And Bradley Cooper Spend Time At A Festival

Footage of the couple dancing emerged on social media, and they look really sweet and comfortable together. A short clip was posted on X by Hadid's fan page, Gigi Hadid Update. But fans of the couple will know this is not the first time they have been seen dancing together.

Earlier this month, they attended Taylor Swift's Eras Tour in Paris, France, alongside Swift's boyfriend, Travis Kelce. Footage emerged of the three of them in a short video posted on X. One of the most recent reports about Hadid and Cooper comes from an ET source who revealed in May 2024 that the couple have been spending time with Swift and Kelce.

"Gigi and Bradley are also excited about their relationship and enjoying this moment and all their time together," the insider claimed. "Taylor, Travis, Gigi, and Bradley have a blast when they get together. They appreciate that they can all be themselves around each other and enjoy double dating and spending time with each other."

They added, "They have a lot of the same interests and have a nice bond, which Taylor and Gigi are so happy about ...Taylor and Gigi have always been extremely close, but being in love simultaneously and having these similar and shared experiences has been so much fun for them."

What Has Been Said About Bradley Cooper And Gigi Hadid's Romance?

Bradley Cooper and Gigi Hadid have been relatively private about their romance, but the footage that has emerged of them together suggests they are in a happy and committed relationship. There have also been reports about how they have bonded over the similarities they share. This includes being single parents.

"They have a lot in common," a source told Us Weekly in October 2023, when they were first linked. "They're both single parents to a young daughter, and they share a similar sense of humor." A source also claimed the model was "really enjoying spending time" with Cooper.
