Keyshia Cole Arrested After Attacking Woman in Birdman's Condo

Publish date: 2024-07-24

Keyshia Cole reportedly visited her boyfriend Birdman early Friday morning and didn't like what she found. The singer, who is dating the CEO of Cash Money, was arrested on Sept. 19 after she attacked a woman she encountered in the rapper's condo, TMZ reports.

 

According to the site, the Grammy-nominated singer showed up to Birdman's home around 5 a.m. and attacked the woman, leaving scratches all over her face.

After being arrested for battery and an outstanding reckless driving warrant on a $46,000 bail, Cole took to her Instagram to post cryptic messages.
