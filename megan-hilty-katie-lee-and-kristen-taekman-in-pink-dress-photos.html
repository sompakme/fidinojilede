Megan Hilty, Katie Lee and Kristen Taekman in Pink Dress: Photos

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Talk about thinking pink! Likeminded ladies Megan Hilty, Katie Lee and Kristen Taekman all slipped into the same J. Jill style. The Yarn-Dyed Linen Buttoned-Hem dress ($89, jjill.com) features a boatneck (perfect for showing off accessories!) and delicate mother-of-pearl buttons that create vents when opened. 

While en route to a New York City recording studio on May 2, Hilty, 35, kept it sweet and simple. The Smash star let her dress do the talking and went without accessories, matching her soft-pink lipstick to the frock. Tan sandals and a ruby-red pedicure completed her look.

Food Network’s Lee, 34, also opted to leave her jewelry at home during a May 4 pool match in Montclair, New Jersey, pairing the dress with lace-up sandals. 

Taekman, 39, wasn’t afraid to add some punch to her pink. In April, while exploring her city, The former Real Housewives of New York City star made the look her own by adding a chunky chain-link collar by Diana Broussard, colorful bracelets, a powder-blue handbag and tan, strappy sandals.
