Hot! Elisabetta Canalis, Mehcad Brooks Go Public With Romance

Publish date: 2024-07-31

Who needs George Clooney with a rebound this hot?

Elisabetta Canalis and new love Mehcad Brooks went public with their romance, arriving together at Monday's launch of the Beauty Book for Brain Cancer in Hollywood.

The Italian model, TV personality and Dancing with the Stars alum, 33, was spotted sweetly fixing the True Blood hunk's tie before they posed for photos together on the red carpet outside the famed Grauman's Chinese Theater.

But Canalis and Brooks, 31, were coy about one another during interviews. (Canalis told Us Weekly "we don't know yet" whether she and her man will spend the holidays together.)

Still, once inside the event, the twosome packed on the PDA and happily posed for affectionate, touchy-feely photos together.
