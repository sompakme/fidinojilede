Stevie Wonder Expecting Triplets, Will Have 11 Children: Report

Publish date: 2024-07-13

Wonder of wonders! At the age of 64, R&B legend Stevie Wonder is preparing to be a daddy yet again. According to Page Six, the "Superstition" singer is expecting triplets with his current girlfriend Tomeeka Robyn Bracy. 

These children will be his ninth, tenth, and eleventh. The soul singer already has children with five different women, and Bracy, 40, gave birth to another child by Wonder last year.
