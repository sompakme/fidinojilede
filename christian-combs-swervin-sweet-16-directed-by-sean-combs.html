Christian Combs Swervin Sweet 16 (Directed By Sean Combs)

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Christian Combs‘ sixteenth birthday was a movie… literally.

When your father is Puff Daddy, there is only one way to do a birthday part: BIG. Puff’s son Christian Combs rung in his 16 birthday earlier this month (Apr. 4) with a star-studded “Swervin’ 16” bash at 1Oak in Los Angeles. With performances by Kendrick Lamar, French Montana and Meek Mill, the youngest of the Combs boys celebrated in style. The party was also attended by the likes Lauren London, Khloe Kardashian, and Faith Evans.

And now, a video montage of Christian’s sixteen birthday — directed by Puff — shows young “KING COMBS” getting ready for the night of his life, featuring some very specific turn up instructions from his pops, and the execution of the them thereafter. Featuring K. Dot’s “Money Trees” and Drake’s “Draft Day” as the score, the 6-minute clip basically upstages any sweet 16 you’ve ever been to.
