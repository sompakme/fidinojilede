First off, Vulture's article pointed to the fact that Affleck and Lopez hadn't been seen together for some time. According to the outlet, Affleck and Lopez were photographed together between March 30th and May 17th 2024.

Vulture also noted that when Lopez attended The 2024 Met Gala, Affleck wasn't by her side.

More importantly, Vulture cited reports that Lopez was solo house hunting in Beverly Hills. The same article pointed to reports that Affleck was staying in a house without Lopez. That house is where Garner was spotted visiting him.

On the other hand, Vulture reported that Affleck and Lopez were seen together on May 17th, 2024. The couple were spotted attending a play that starred Fin Affleck, according to TMZ. That said, TMZ reported that after the play, Affleck dropped Lopez and Fin Affleck off at the family mansion and returned to his separate home.

Finally and possibly most importantly, Vulture cited a report from US Weekly. That report cited two insiders who said Lopez and Affleck are working on their relationship to avoid getting separated or divorced.
