Everything Vanna White Spends Her $70 Million Net Worth On

Publish date: 2024-09-26

Vanna White will forever be known as the letter-turning stunner on The Wheel Of Fortune, and she has amassed an astronomical $70 million net worth in the process. Fans spanning across a variety of different age groups have all tuned in to see her consistent presence on the show, and many have drooled over the absolutely stunning array of dresses she has worn on the show.

Good Housekeeping reports that her steady appearance on this long-running hit game show has truly afforded White the ability to live her life any way she'd like and her flexible working schedule means she has truly been able to enjoy the fruits of her labor.

9 Fun Adventures

As part of her travels, Vanna White makes sure to engage in a series of fun adventures. Without finances holding back, she's able to participate in all aspects of fun that each location has to offer to her. Her social media accounts are riddled with exciting activities that most of us would absolutely love to take part in.

She's been able to spend time with the monkeys, been up close and personal with some giraffes, explored the Swiss Alps and has even been on the track during the car races. If there's a spot she wants to discover and an adventure that can be had, Vanna White is prepared to drop the cash in pursuit of fun.

8 Golfing

Vanna White loves golfing, and she drops cash to golf anywhere and everywhere she goes. She's constantly challenging herself in the sport and continues to pursue the perfect golf swing during her time off at home, as well as when she is away on vacation. When it comes to golf, she's all-in, and she enjoys documenting her progress, too. Of course, there's no doubt that she keeps stock of the very best when it comes to clubs and accessories, too.

7 Real Estate

Vanna White lives life in the lap of luxury, in a very, very big way. She has an enormous mansion that is worth well over $47 million and boasts every imaginable perk and upgrade imaginable. Her not-so-humble abode looks more like a resort than a residence, and she has taken guests on a tour via the internet to highlight the many features of her home.

Vanna's expansive real estate is as impressive as it is expensive, and it is a reflection of her incredible wealth.

6 World Travels

One of the most incredible things Vanna White has spent her fortune on, is her absolute free ability to travel as she wishes. Her enormous bank account has given her the opportunity to travel the world, and she has definitely been able to reap the benefits of her travels.

She loves the beach and has been to some of the most spectacular beaches the world has to offer. Her Instagram account features trips to Italy, Amsterdam, Hawaii, Scotland, and so many more incredible vacation spots. She loves exploring the world and has the bank account to be able to support a trip to any location her heart desires.

5 Family

Family is everything to Vanna White. She has greatly enjoyed the benefits of a very flexible work schedule, which has enabled her to spend increased amounts of time with her loved ones. Vanna only works four days a month. It's hard to imagine earning the incredible income that she has for such minimal time spent at work, but this is Vanna's life, and she's enjoying it to the fullest!

White's social media account is a tell-all about the importance of her family, and she spends freely on making memories with them. She has snapped a series of images with her son, depicting wild back-stage photos at concert events, and a whole lot of cool moments that only Vanna could afford on a whim.

4 Theater

One of Vanna's guilty pleasures is the theater, and she keeps an entertainment budget ready for this extra curricular fun. Of course, the best seats in the house are always hers, and she has the ability to head to the theater to catch any and every production that she's interested in. She's been known to go with her son, as well as with her friends, and she boasts her experiences online, to share with her adoring fans.

3 Gambling

According to media reports, Vanna White happens to have a passion for gambling, and she does so often. She does, however, admit to putting herself on a tight budget when she gambles, to ensure that she doesn't get too carried away. She limits herself to just $100 each time she goes in to a casino, and shockingly in spite of that tiny little budget, she happens to do very well, thanks to her lucky streak!

She says; “I gamble for fun... like going to the tables in Vegas and saying, OK, I’m gonna take this hundred dollars, I’m gonna play on the five-dollar table, and I’m gonna gamble. For me, it’s a hundred dollars of entertainment. It’s worth the entertainment thinking, you know, I hate losing, but sometimes I lose, sometimes I win.”

Of course, she drew a Wheel Of Fortune reference and went on to say; “Luck is luck, and sometimes you spin the wheel, and you might get bankrupt every time.”

2 Concerts

Much like many of her fans, Vanna White is passionate about music and is known to drop big bucks on the very best seats to all the concerts she attends. As far as the types of concerts she goes to - well - her music taste seems to vary and her social media accounts highlight various music genres. She has attended county music concerts, rock concerts, and everything in between, and tends to travel to each big event with someone she loves - often with her son.

1 Sports Entertainment

Don't be fooled by all the pretty gowns she wears on the set of The Wheel Of Fortune... this lovely lady also has a sporty side!  Vanna White is a huge baseball fan and can be seen rooting for the Dodgers at every opportunity. She spends a good chunk of change attending as many games as her schedule allows and posts about her love of the team and the sport quite often.
