A Look At All The Mansions Owned By Cardi B

Belcalis Marlenis Almánzar, aka Cardi B, is among the biggest names in hip-hop and pop music today. Since bursting onto the scene in 2015, she has taken the world by storm with hit songs like Bodak Yellow, I Like It, and WAP. With her vibrant personality, unique style, and undeniable talent, Cardi B has won multiple awards, including a Grammy for Best Rap Album in 2019. Her meteoric rise to fame has also led to enormous financial success. Today, Cardi B boasts a stunning $80 million net worth, with a significant portion of that wealth invested into an impressive portfolio of luxury real estate.

Cardi B owns homes in some of the most exclusive neighborhoods across the United States. Her property collection is estimated to be worth at least $12 million, including lavish mansions in Atlanta and New York City.

The Extravagant New York Mansion

Being a New York native, Cardi B knew she wanted a place to call home in her hometown. In 2017, she purchased a lavish mansion in the suburbs of Garden State for $5.85 million, with an additional $1 Million put into renovations, as per NY Post. The 13,000 square-foot French-style chateau sits on over 6 acres of land and boasts nine bedrooms and nine bathrooms. No expense was spared on the interiors, with marble floors, chandeliers, and a sweeping staircase greeting guests in the grand foyer.

The chef's kitchen is a food lover's paradise with top-of-the-line appliances, marble countertops, and a large island that seats up to 8 people. There's a home theater, wine cellar, cigar room, full bar, and lounge area with a pool table for entertainment. Outdoors, the resort-style grounds feature a large in-ground swimming pool with an outdoor kitchen, a tennis court, a playground, and manicured gardens. This suburban oasis gives Cardi B plenty of space to relax and entertain when she's not in the city.

The Palatial Atlanta Estate

In 2019, Cardi B and her husband, Offset, purchased a jaw-dropping mega-mansion in Atlanta for $5.8 million. Nestled in an exclusive gated community, this 22,000-square-foot modern farmhouse sits on nearly five acres of land, as per House Digest. The exterior features floor-to-ceiling windows, a covered patio, and a detached four-car garage. Soaring 2-story ceilings, marble floors, and minimalist decor create an airy, open-concept layout. The great room alone spans over 3,000 square feet and includes a marble fireplace, wet bar, and lounge seating.

The mansion features five bedrooms, including a massive primary suite with dual bathrooms and walk-in closets. For leisure, amenities include a home theater, gym, spa, 2000-bottle capacity wine cellar, cigar lounge, and an elevator to access all three floors. Outdoors, the resort-style grounds have a picturesque pool and a tennis court. This Atlanta estate provides plenty of amenities for Cardi B and her family to enjoy a luxurious lifestyle.

The Lavish Edgewater Condominium

Realizing her childhood dream to buy her mother a house, Cardi B purchased a lavish mansion in New Jersey for $1.5 million in 2018, as per Love Property. Located in Cardi's hometown of Edgewater, this stunning waterfront estate has eight bedrooms and bathrooms with multiple amenities. It features a gourmet chef's kitchen with stainless steel appliances, a dark earth-toned backsplash, and a central island with a marble countertop.

The living room has high ceilings, a marble fireplace, and custom built-ins opening to the backyard. Upstairs is a lavish primary suite with dual walk-in closets, spa bathrooms, and a private terrace overlooking the NYC skyline. Other amenities included a modified basement featuring a gym and a large entertaining space. This luxurious estate is the perfect gift for Cardi to provide her mother with a comfortable home near her family.

Dominican Republic Property

Cardi B’s husband, Offset, gifted her a stunning beachfront villa in the Dominican Republic worth over $1.45 million, according to Dirt. Located in the exclusive gated community in Las Terrenas, this 7,000-square-foot villa sits on over 4 acres of oceanfront land. It has six bedrooms, six bathrooms and can sleep up to 14 guests - perfect for Cardi to vacation with friends and family.

The interiors feature marble floors, exposed wood beam ceilings and are decorated in a coastal chic style. The gourmet kitchen and adjacent dining patio are ideal for entertaining. Outdoors, palm trees and vibrant gardens surround the villa leading to a private beach. Amenities include an infinity pool, a small bedroom pool fire pit, a covered patio, an Italian kitchen, and direct beach access. This Caribbean oasis offers Cardi B a secluded and luxurious vacation home to relax and recharge.

From modern mega-mansions to lavish tropical villas, Cardi B’s homes provide plenty of amenities for living a luxurious life. Cardi’s luxurious real estate purchases showcase her meteoric rise to fame and savvy investments in securing comfortable homes for herself and her loved ones.
