Beyonce Channels a Christmas Tree in Festive Holiday Outfit

Publish date: 2024-09-17

Rockin’ around the ‘Yonce tree! Beyoncé was in the holiday spirit dressed as a Christmas tree to attend a festive party in NYC on Monday, December 14.

For Bey, 34, it was the season to layer her striped midi dress with a sparkly plaid poncho that was bedecked with tinsel, ornaments, lights and stockings. She even accessorized with a Santa hat and tree sunglasses. Hoop earrings and combat boots completed her Tannenbaum-themed style.
