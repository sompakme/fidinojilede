Taylor Swift's Fans Would Rather Talk About The Video Than Congratulate Blake Lively's Directorial D

Publish date: 2024-07-06

Known for her teasers and song and video guessing games, Taylor Swift has once again released a short trailer to her upcoming music video for a song off of Red (Taylor's Version). Actress Blake Lively will be making her directorial debut, and has also been credited as a co-writer. However, following the trailer release, congratulating Lively is not what most fans are thinking of doing.

Following the teaser, Swift confirmed that the video will be for her song "I Bet You Think About Me (Taylor's Version)." Twitter has been celebrating the announcement of the video, and has since posted their excitement for its premiere.

Although "I Bet You Think About Me (Taylor's Version)" will be what the video is for, Twitter has continued to play song guessing games. Following her social media post, most users have guessed the music video to be for "Blank Space," "betty," and "Message In A Bottle." Interestingly enough, "Blank Space" and "betty" are songs that were not included in Red or Red (Taylor's Version).

"All Too Well" Became A Hit Sensation

The hype for her next music video comes shortly after Swift released All Too Well: The Short Film, starring Dylan O'Brien and Sadie Sink. Supporting the longer version of her song "All Too Well," the video received positive reviews. Brittany Spanos of Rolling Stone called it a "dramatic and moving film that digs deep into heartache and scarf lore."

As of this publication, the video has received over 22 million views, and has been the number one trending video on YouTube for the first 48 hours after its release. O'Brien has also added (Taylor's Version) to his Twitter account after the video premiere, and the video recently played during Swift's debut performance of the song on Saturday Night Live.

Not Entirely Forgotten By The Fans

Although Swifties have not congratulated the A Simple Favor actress for her participation in the video, her fans have begun to show love for her on Twitter. Some have also brought up rumors that this video could feature her husband Ryan Reynolds, or show hints of their marriage, or previous relationships Lively was in before meeting him.

Swift's music video will premiere on Nov. 15 at 10:00 AM. Due to most of her videos debuting at 10:00 AM, it is likely that there will not be a surprise early release. Red and Red (Taylor's Version) is currently available to stream on Spotify and Apple Music.
