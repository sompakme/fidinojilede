Kelly Osbourne News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-07-07

Name:Kelly Osbourne

Relationship:Dating

Birthday:October 27, 1984

About

Daughter of legendary rocker Ozzy Osbourne, Kelly Osbourne grew up traveling on tour with her father and lived in more than 20 homes between the U.K. and the U.S.

Her first TV debut was with her family in the reality series The Osbournes, which won an Emmy for Outstanding Reality Program in 2002.

That same year, Osbourne released an album called Shut Up!, which was first heard at the 2002 MTV Movie Awards. Though the album received bad reviews, Osbourne went on to record a duet with her father called “Changes.”

In 2004, Osbourne began an acting career with a starring role in ABC’s Life as We Know It. She went on to appear in The Queen’s Handbag, Live Freaky Die Freaky, Sharknado 2: The Second One and the TBS sitcom Are We There Yet? In 2007, Osbourne made her stage debut as the prison matron in the musical Chicago at the Cambridge Theatre in London.

Osbourne placed third on ABC’s reality competition series Dancing With the Stars in 2009 with pro partner Louis van Amstel. She competed on season 2 of The Masked Singer in 2019, coming in ninth.

The TV personality dated model Luke Worrall starting in 2008, but the couple called it quits — and ended their engagement — in July 2010. The following year, Osbourne met vegan baker Matthew Mosshart at Kate Moss‘ wedding. The two got engaged in 2013, only to announce their amicable split the following year. The Fashion Police alum dated cinematographer Erik Bragg in 2020, but they broke up after one year. Osbourne announced in February 2022 that she is dating DJ Sidney George Wilson. That May, she revealed she is expecting her first child though did not share who is the father.
