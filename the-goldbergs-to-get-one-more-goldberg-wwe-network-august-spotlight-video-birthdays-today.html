ABC’s The Goldbergs To Get One More Goldberg

PWInisder.com is reporting that Goldberg has booked a role on ABC’s popular comedy The Goldbergs.

The sitcom is set in the 1980s and follows a family from the Philadelphia area. Goldberg will be playing the brother of Coach Mueller who is the gym teacher at the high school that the kids from the family attend.

The creator of the show, Adam Goldberg, is a big pro wrestling fan. The episode is set to film shortly as Goldberg participated in a table read of the script this week.
