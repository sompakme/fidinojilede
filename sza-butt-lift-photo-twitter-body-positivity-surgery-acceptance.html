The division on body positivity & surgery Acceptance

Moreover, they also argue that plastic surgery can be a dangerous and expensive procedure with potential health risks and complications.

On the other hand, advocates of surgery acceptance argue that people have the right to make their own choices regarding their bodies and should not be shamed for wanting to alter or enhance their appearance. 

They believe that cosmetic surgery can be a liberating and empowering experience that boosts confidence and self-esteem. 

They also argue that women, in particular, are often subjected to unrealistic body standards and should have the freedom to enhance their bodies on their own terms.
