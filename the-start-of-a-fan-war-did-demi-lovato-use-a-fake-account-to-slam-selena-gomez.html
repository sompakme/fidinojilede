The Start Of A Fan War... Did Demi Lovato Use A Fake Account To Slam Selena Gomez?

Publish date: 2024-08-01

The news spread like wildfire when in a recent interview with Demi Lovato revealed that she wasn't close with Selena Gomez. Now, another rumor has been passed, accusing Lovato of creating a fake Instagram account also known as a 'Finsta' to hate on the Rare singer.

Fans claimed to have discovered an Instagram account which was allegedly used by Lovato to spread negative comments about Selena Gomez.

#SorryNotSorry?

On a casual Friday afternoon, a collage of photos from the assumed fake social media account leaked online. The Finsta's account handle is @traumaqueen4eva and the feed is packed with photos of Demi and Selena, with vile captions thrown at Gomez. One of the posts includes the former star holding up a cover of People's magazine that has Selena and Demi on the cover but Selena's face has deliberately been scribbled out.

The caption reads, "You can call me Perri Labelle."

According to The Blast, the hashtag #DemiLovatoIsOverParty very quickly began to trend on twitter and ultimately, Lovato was accused of being a fake towards Gomez and other celebrities.

#DemiLovatoIsOverParty

Nonetheless, all hope was not lost for Lovato as many of her fans came to her defense and claimed that the account was fake. On the other side of this, Selena Gomezs' fans say otherwise.

"Lovatics keep saying it's fake, but if it's fake then why did demi disable comments on her main Insta, remove everyone from her followers, change the username, and deactivated instead of defending herself?" A fan wrote.

A Lovatic responded to the comment above, "#DemiLovatoIsOverParty is trending because Selena Gomez's fans are once again being spiteful, spreading FAKE screenshots of Demi's finsta."

Social media is now in the midst of a fan war between Lovatics and Selenators. Hopefully, this feud would end and the truth shall come out. Vulture reports that because even though Lovato isn't friends with Gomez, she still; "loves her and wishes everybody nothing but the best."
