Dwyane Wade is a retired professional basketball player who played in the National Basketball Association (NBA) for 16 seasons playing for the Miami Heat. He was born on January 17, 1982, in Chicago, Illinois. He played for the Heat most of his career, winning three NBA championships with the team in 2006, 2012, and 2013.

Dwyane Wade is known not only for His NBA career but also for his love for the top of range cars. The NBA all-star has acquired an impressive collection of vehicles ranging from the Ferrari F12 Berlinetta to the Jeep Wrangler. Wade is also engaged in several charitable and entrepreneurial endeavors outside of the NBA, including co-founding the Wade clothing brand and working as a TV analyst for TNT's NBA coverage. This article will take a closer look at Dwayne Wade's car collection.

Ferrari F12 Berlinetta

Via Wikimedia Commons

Ferrari F12 Berlinetta is one of Wade's favorite cars in his collection. The Ferrari F12 Berlinetta boasts a 6.3-litre V12 engine that can produce up to 731 horsepower, allowing it to go from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.1 seconds. Ferrari manufactures this state-of-the-art sports car with a top speed of over 211 miles per hour. Ferrari F12 Berlinetta is a stunning example of Ferrari engineering and design, combining exceptional performance with beautiful styling and advanced technology.It is estimated to cost $270,000.

McLaren MP4-12C

Via Wikimedia Commons

The McLaren MP4-12C features a 3.8-litre twin-turbo V8 engine that can produce up to 592 horsepower. This British-manufactured high-performance sports car has a seven-speed dual-clutch and can reach a top speed of over 200 miles per hour. MP4-12C remains a popular and sought-after super-car among car enthusiasts and collectors.It is estimated to cost $230,000.

Porsche 911 GT3 RS

Via Wikimedia Commons

This car is a track-focused version of the Porsche 911, and Wade has added it to his collection. This high-performance sports car boasts a 4.0-litre six-cylinder engine that produces 502 horsepower and 346 lb-ft of torque. Its engine is a seven-speed dual–clutch automatic transmission which can reach over 200 miles per hour. Porsche 911 GT3 RS is also a limited-production model, which makes it a highly sought-after and exclusive vehicle.It is estimated to cost $84,000.

Mercedes-Benz SLS AMG

Via Wikimedia Commons

The SLS AMG is a gull-winged sports car favorite among car enthusiasts. It features a 6.2-litre V8 engine that can produce up to 563 horsepower. It is a luxury car manufactured by German automakers and can reach over 199 miles per hour. It's so powerful it can accelerate from 0 to 60 miles in just 3.7 seconds. Due to its limited production, the car is a sought–after collector's item.The cost is estimated to be over $200,000.

Ford Bronco

Via Wikimedia Commons

The Ford Bronco is a sport utility vehicle (SUV) that the Ford Motor Company first introduced in 1965. The Bronco is a multipurpose vehicle that can manage city driving and off-road conditions. It quickly gained a reputation as a rugged and dependable vehicle that was ideal for outdoor enthusiasts. This car was a gift from Wade Hollywood star wife, Gabrielle Union, on Father's day.

Jeep Wrangler

Via Wikimedia Commons

This car is more practical in Wade's collection, perfect for cruising around Miami. Wade has customized his Wrangler with a matte black finish, black rims, and an upgraded sound system.It is known for its rugged design and off-road capabilities, as well as its open-air driving experience with its removable roof and doors.

The Hummer H2 SUT

Via Wikimedia Commons

The Hummer H2 SUT Is a Sport Utility Truck powered by a 6.0-litre V8 engine that produces 325 horsepower and 365 lb-ft of torque. The Hummer H2 SUT has a maximum towing capacity of 8,200 pounds and comes standard with a four-speed automatic transmission and full-time four-wheel drive.The cost is estimated to be $160,000.

Cadillac Escalade EXT

Via Wikimedia Commons

The Cadillac Escalade is a luxury SUV car manufactured by General Motors. This car was first introduced in 1999 and has undergone four generations of redesigns.it is known for its impressive towing capability. It has a 6.2-litre V8 engine produces 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque. The Cadillac Escalade is a luxurious, powerful SUV with advanced technology and impressive performance capabilities.It is estimated to cost $120,000.

Ferrari GTC4Lusso

Via Wikimedia Commons

Ferrari GTC4Lusso is also a favorite in Wade's car collection. It is a high-performance, four-seater grand tourer car produced by the Italian automaker Ferrari. The GTC4Lusso features a front-mounted V12 engine producing 680 horsepower, and it can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.4 seconds, with a top speed of over 200 mph. Its comfort and luxury make it popular among people who demand power and style.It is estimated to cost $270,000.
