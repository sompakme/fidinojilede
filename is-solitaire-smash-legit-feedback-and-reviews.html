To join in the fun and excitement of Solitaire Smash, players must familiarize themselves with the rules and gameplay. The objective of the game is to clear the board by building sequences of cards in the foundations and the tableau. It follows the same rules as traditional solitaire, ensuring that players can easily transition into the game.

Timing is crucial in Solitaire Smash, as players are rewarded for clearing the board as quickly as possible. To score higher, players can aim for big time bonuses by undoing moves and strategizing their gameplay. The top three players in each tournament are rewarded with real money or gems, adding an extra incentive for players to showcase their solitaire skills.

Solitaire Smash also offers daily rewards and special events to keep players engaged. With the chance to win real cash prizes and exciting gameplay features, Solitaire Smash brings a fresh and competitive twist to the classic solitaire game.
