Indian film actor and producer Sanjay Dutt has an estimated net worth of $10 million. He earned his net worth for through his work in the Hindi film industry.

Sanjay Balraj Dutt was born on July 29, 1959 in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. He is the son of Bollywood film greats Sunil and Nargis Dutt, and made his film debut in the 1981 Bollywood film Rocky, which was directed by his father.

Three days before the premier of his debut movie, Sanjay Dutt’s mother died. This motivated him to become a star, leading him to appear in high-profile films such as the 1982 movie Vidhaata, the highest-grossing film of that year. Wider recognition came when he took the lead role in the 1986 film Naam.

Sanjay Dutt continued to be successful in the 1990s, appearing in multiple cult classic films, though several altercations with the law made him a controversial figure. In one instance, Dutt was arrested on charges alleging involvement in the 1993 Mumbai bombings. He served four years in prison.

Dutt made a comeback in the late nineties, and appeared in many other cult films including Daud, Dushma, Vaastay: The Reality, and Jodi No.1. Along with IPL cricket team owner Raj Kundra, he started the first mixed martial arts team in India in the early part of 2012.
